UTags - Add custom tags and notes to users, posts, and videos across the web. Enhance your browsing experience by organizing content, identifying users, and filtering out unwanted posts. Perfect for personalizing your web experience and improving productivity.

Key Features:
- Add custom tags to users, posts, videos, and links
- Filter and hide content with special tags (block, ignore, hide)
- Mark visited content automatically
- Export/import your tag data
- Modern bookmark management with web interface at https://utags.link/
- Works seamlessly across 100+ popular websites
